A Appendix 1 - notation & abbreviations

A.1 Notation

For the hypothetical substance, X:

  • \(\text{plasma concentration }= P_{X}\)
  • \(\text{urine concentration }= U_{X}\)
  • \(\text{clearance }= C_{X}\)
  • \(\text{exchangeable X }= X_{e}\)
